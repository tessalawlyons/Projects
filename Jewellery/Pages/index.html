Life on Mars Jewellery shop!

Welcome to my jewellery shop! I sell handmade earrings, necklaces, keyrings, and anything else you can put a charm on. I make most of my charms myself from polymer clay, so you can ask me to make anything you’d like! Just fill in a custom order form, or alternatively choose one of my premade products.

All my products are sanitised and cleansed before shipping, and if you order more than one product, shipping is free! All orders also come with a little thank you, and if anything is broken, not up to scratch or you simply don’t like it, I’ll refund you fully or send you something else, just contact me.
